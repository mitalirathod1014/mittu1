Bharat: Sunil Grover joins the cast of Salman Khan and Priyanka Chopra's film


li Abbas Zafar's Bharat has a new addition to its cast: Sunil Grover. The actor-comedian will play a pivotal role in the period drama, the director announced in a statement. Reports suggest that Sunil will play Salman Khan's friend in the film.

"I am very excited to welcome Sunil Grover to the 'Bharat' family. Sunil Grover is one of the most loved names in the Television industry, he has an immense fan following owing to his commendable work. 'Bharat' has him playing a very pivotal part in the film and he along with Salman Khan is sure to create magic on screen," Ali said.

Bharat will mark Priyanka Chopra's comeback in Bollywood. After the 2016 film Jai Gangajal, Priyanka had been busy with her Hollywood ventures, and fans are excited about her return to home turf. Director Ali Abbas Zafar said in an interview that her character will be "the soul of the film".

An official adaptation of 2014 South Korean film, Ode to My Father, Bharat will reportedly feature Salman in five different looks. It is said that the actor's Maine Pyar Kiya look would also be recreated for this film.

Produced by Atul Agnihotri's Reel Life Production and Bhushan Kumar's T-Series, Bharat will hit the screens on Eid 2019.
